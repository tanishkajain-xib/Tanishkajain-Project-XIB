Tourism

Arunachal Pradesh:

  • Trekking routes (e.g., Siang Valley Trek)
  • Cultural heritage (e.g., Tawang Monastery)
  • Natural beauty (e.g., Namdapha National Park)

Sikkim:

  • Scenic beauty (e.g., Kanchenjunga National Park)
  • Trekking routes (e.g., Singalila National Park)
  • Cultural heritage (e.g., Rumtek Monastery)

Arunachal Pradesh: 10,000 tourist arrivals (2020)

Sikkim: 50,000 tourist arrivals (2020)

Agriculture

Arunachal Pradesh:

  • Rice production (2.5 lakh metric tons, 2020)
  • Horticulture crops (e.g., apples, oranges)

Sikkim:

  • Organic farming (100% organic state)
  • Cardamom production (1,500 metric tons, 2020)

Arunachal Pradesh: 60% of population engaged in agriculture

Sikkim: 70% of population engaged in agriculture

Industries

Arunachal Pradesh:

  • Cement production (2 million metric tons, 2020)
  • Power generation (1,000 MW, 2020)

Sikkim:

  • Pharmaceuticals (e.g., Cipla, Sun Pharma)
  • Food processing (e.g., fruit processing)

Arunachal Pradesh: 20% growth in industrial sector (2020)

Sikkim: 15% growth in industrial sector (2020)
